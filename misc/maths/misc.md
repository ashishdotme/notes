# Random Formulas

## Inverse a Number
Mod(%) operation on a number by 10 returns the rightmost digit
Divide(/) operation on a number by 10 removes the rightmost digit

Num = 126

$Num = (1*100) + (2*10) + (6)$

-----------
Reversing 126 % 10 = 6  
int(126/10) = 12  
12 % 10 = 2  
int(12/10) = 1  
1 % 10 = 1  

$Reverse = (6*100) + (2*10) + (1)$
